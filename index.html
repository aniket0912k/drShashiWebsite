Dr. Shashi's

Highlights

Dr. Shashi Sharma is a renowned figure in the realm of education, with an impressive portfolio of achievements and contributions. Holding various esteemed positions, Dr. Sharma's dedication to shaping the educational landscape is truly commendable.

  • Director (DVM Group of Schools): Dr. Sharma serves as the Deputy Director for the chain of K-12 schools in the DVM Group. Her role in this honorary position involves providing valuable insights and guidance to ensure the growth and excellence of the schools.
  • ( Empowered Minds Edu-Solutions): As the Director of Empowered Minds Edu-Solutions, Dr. Sharma plays a pivotal role in shaping innovative educational solutions that empower young minds for a brighter future.
  • President (Maria Montessori Group of Schools): Dr. Sharma holds an honorary Vice President position in the esteemed Maria Montessori Group of Schools, contributing her expertise to the development and success of these preschools.
  • Vice Chairperson (Children's Education Trust): Dr. Sharma's involvement as the Vice Chairperson in the Children's Education Trust, established by the CEO of the DAV Group, showcases her commitment to enhancing children's education on a broader scale.
  • Managing Director, EDUTalk: Dr. Sharma's leadership as the Managing Director of EDUTalk demonstrates her passion for meaningful conversations and insights in the education sector.
  • Chief Secretary - FLARE: Dr. Sharma's role as Chief Secretary of FLARE underscores her dedication to fostering collaboration and positive change in the educational sphere.
  • National General Sec Private School and Children Welfare Association: As the National General Secretary of this Association, Dr. Sharma contributes to the advancement of private schools and the overall welfare of children.
  • Accreditation Project for Pre-Schools: Dr. Sharma's expertise in designing accreditation projects for pre-schools reflects her commitment to ensuring high-quality early education.
  • Curriculum Design Expert: Known for her proficiency in designing pre-primary and primary school curricula, Dr. Sharma's influence extends to crafting educational journeys that inspire and empower.
  • School Establishments: Dr. Sharma has successfully established two new schools under the Greenberry Group in Gaziabad, one Prestige Junior School in Rohini, and one Progresso School in Faridabad.
