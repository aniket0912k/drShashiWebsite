Experiences

Dr. Shashi Sharma is a renowned figure in the realm of education, with an impressive portfolio of achievements and contributions. Holding various esteemed positions, Dr. Sharma's dedication to shaping the educational landscape is truly commendable.

  • Co-Founder/Vice-President at Time2Grow Media Pvt Ltd (December 2021-Present): Continues to co-lead a media company that aims to facilitate growth and progress.
  • Country Head at Petals (Group of Schools), New Delhi (Dec 2017-Dec 2019): Demonstrated exceptional leadership as the Country Head of Petals Group of Schools.
  • Managing Director at EDUTalk organization (January 2017 - December 2021): Led EDUTalk with a vision to enrich educational discourse.
  • Founder Principal at Mother's Pride/Presidium (Primary School), Punjab (October 2016 - December 2017): Established a strong foundation as the Founder Principal of Mother's Pride/Presidium Primary School in Punjab.
  • Sr. School Board Academic Advisor cum Project Manager at American Board (NWAC Board cum Accreditation Agency), New Delhi (Jan 2016 - September 2016):Contributed expertise as an academic advisor and project manager.
  • Founder Principal at The KiDS Square- Pride International School and Bali International School, New Delhi (Feb 2014 - Jan 2016): Pioneered educational excellence as the Founder Principal.
  • Director at The KiDS Square Pvt. Ltd, Head Office, New Delhi (Feb 2014 - Jan 2016): Demonstrated leadership as a Director at the head office of The KiDS Square Pvt. Ltd.
